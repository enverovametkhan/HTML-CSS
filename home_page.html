Welcome to your home dashboard of Ametkhan!
Here is a brief explanation on how you can use the platform:

Browsing Demo Sprites

  • View our demo Sprites from "Samples" tab
  • Can copy them into your workspace, and they will appear under "Sprites" tab
  • Then you can browse through their "Knowledge Bases", "Workflows", or even chat to them - be aware those Sprites are for illustration and browsing purposes only

Creating Sprites

  • In "Sprites" tab, click on "Add Sprite", choose a for your new Sprite - it will then redirect you to Sprites configuration page
  • Create Knowledge Bases under "Base Knowledge" tab
  • Create Workflows under "Workflows" tab
  • Chat and test your sprite under "Chat" tab
  • Activate your Sprite for everyone in the workspace under "Deploy" tab
  • Make your sprite Public for anyone with internetunder "Deploy" tab
  • Add your sprite to your website under "Deploy" tab
  • Change logo for your Sprite under "Settings" tab - available to premium workspaces only

Adding members to workspace

  • In "Workspace" tab, click on "Members"
  • Make sure you have sufficient credits to add a new member. It costs 30 SpriteAI Credits per new member per month
  • Type new member's email onto the field and click on "Add Email"
  • It will send an invitation email with furter instruction

    • Permission levels

    • An "Owner" of the workplace has full access and rights to everything within a workspace
    • An "Admin" of the workplace has the same access and rights as the "Owner" except Deleting Workspace or Sprites
    • A "User" of the workplace can only chat to Sprites within a workspace

If you require further assistance please reach out by clicking on "Support" tab on the left side menu
